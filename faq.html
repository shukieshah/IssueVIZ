Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the different modes?

Histogram displays the key election topics for the year in a bar chart format, making it easier for viewers to clearly see the order of topics in frequency, as well as the number of times the topic was referenced. Viewers can click on the top 10 issues to see more details.

Bubble View breaks down topics into "key words", where a topic may be associated with more than one word. This provides the viewer with a more specific breakdown of topics, by allowing them to hover on different words and see its frequency.

Word Cloud provides an interesting visualization for viewers to look at topics. They can associate the size of the word with the relative frequency, providing stark contrast between high-frequency and low-frequency topics.

Trends displays all the top topics at once, showing its evolution over a time. Here, viewers can see which topics became more or less important throughout the years, and which ones stayed the same. They can use the selection tool to pick and choose which topics to look at.



Why Reddit data?

Not only is Reddit data easier to extract and work with, Reddit as a platform encourages more discussion, and applying this data can provide more insight into what people are talking about in a more in-depth manner. Reddit has specific subreddits targeted towards political discussion that can be isolated to get a look at what topics are important in politics and the election.



Why is there only data starting from 2008?

Reddit, and Internet discourse in general, had not been popular or plentiful before the 2008 election. While we cannot gather data before 2008, these four election years still provide meaningful data for us to analyze.



What are the takeaways of this tool?

This tool is a visualization for you, as a viewer, to take a look at what issues plague the nation and what issues voters are concerned with. You will be able to see how these topics change - or maybe, do not change - over time. You may be surprised at what issues are important to Americans. You may ask yourself if candidates are addressing the issues that they should be, or if they need to shift their focus. This data is an informational starting point for you to recognize the key topics, but we also hope to pique your own curiosity in the elections going forward.
